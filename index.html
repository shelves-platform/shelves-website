Shelves is an intuitive cross platform application that helps users save time, money, and the environment by reducing food waste.

Save Time

Our smart inventory management lets you know what's at home - and what you need to pick up at the store.

Stop Throwing Away Money

Item expiration alerts let you know what's going to spoil - before it actually does.

Reduce Waste

Join us in our efforts to help save the environment by reducing food & packing waste.

Easy Inventory Management

Add items to Shelves by scanning a barcode, searching for an item, or manually entering it.

Standardized Swipe Gestures

Want to interact with an item? Just swipe left or right to take action. For example, did you just polish off that last bit of orange juice in the fridge? Mark it as consumed with a quick swipe!

Advanced Options

Don't see what you're looking for when you swipe? Just tap on an item to see more options. Shelves offers quantity adjustments, shopping list modifications, and more!
