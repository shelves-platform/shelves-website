Shelves App

Shelves is an intuitive cross platform application that helps users save time, money, and the environment by reducing food waste.

Save Time

Our smart inventory management lets you know what's at home - and what you need to pick up at the store.

Stop Throwing Away Money

Item expiration alerts let you know what's going to spoil - before it actually does.

Reduce Waste

Join us in our efforts to help save the environment by reducing food & packing waste.

Complete Inventory Management

Track all of your household's food easily by sorting it with tags within the application.

Several Ways To Add

Add items to Shelves by scanning a barcode, searching for an item in our database, or manually entering it.

Develop Smarter Shopping Lists

Create & check off shopping lists within the app. Shelves can help to make sure you are always remembering to buy your favorites with our essentials feature.

Get Notified Before It's Too Late

Stop wasting your hard earned money. Shelves alerts you before things are about to go bad. You can even customize these alerts to your liking.

Organize Multiple Areas

Manage all your home's fridges, freezers, and pantries within the application.

Involve The Whole Household

Doesn't matter if you have a spouse, family, or roommate - share & manage the food contents of your home with others!
